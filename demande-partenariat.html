WAGEDO is an initiative committed to promoting sustainable development, social innovation, and economic empowerment through impactful community projects. We believe that significant changes arise from collaboration, which is why we invite organizations, businesses, institutions, and development actors to become partners of WAGEDO.

By joining our network of partners, you contribute to:

  • Strengthening social impact through concrete actions on the ground.
  • Supporting local innovation in agriculture, education, technology, and the environment.
  • Creating sustainable synergies between public and private stakeholders.
  • Enhancing your social commitment through visible and measurable projects.
We are open to different types of partnerships:
  • Technical partnerships (support, training, expertise)
  • Financial partnerships (project support, grants, sponsorship)
  • Institutional and strategic partnerships
Together, let’s build a sustainable and supportive future with WAGEDO.
For any collaboration or proposal, please fill out our partnership request form.
