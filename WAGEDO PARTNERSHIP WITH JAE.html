WAGEDO PARTNERSHIP WITH JAE (Jeune Acteur de l'Énergie)

As part of its action plan, WAGEDO has placed strong emphasis on building partnerships to expand its impact. In January 2025, WAGEDO signed a key partnership with Jeunes Acteurs de l’énergie (JAE), an organization based in Burkina Faso that works in clean energy and environmental protection. This collaboration represents a major step in strengthening WAGEDO visibility and demonstrates its commitment to working alongside organizations with shared values and goals in the energy sector.
