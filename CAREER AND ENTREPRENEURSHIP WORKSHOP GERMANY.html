CAREER & ENTREPRENEURSHIP WORKSHOPS FOR IMP-EGH STUDENTS - GERMANY

From 12 to 13 June, 2025, WAGEDO took part in the Career and Entrepreneurship Workshops for IMP-EGH students. The event was held at Forschungszentrum Jülich and the Startup Village NRW at Brainenergy Park in Germany, and served as a dynamic platform to connect students, academia, and industry stakeholders. The workshops brought together 67 talented master’s students, alongside representatives from RWTH Aachen University, Rostock University, and the WASCAL Management. Through interactive sessions, participants explored opportunities in career development, entrepreneurship, and innovation in the hydrogen and energy sectors. WAGEDO, through its initiatives, actively contributed by sharing its own experiences in entrepreneurship with the students, offering insights into practical challenges and opportunities in the clean energy space.
